Who we are

Scirobnics Technology Pvt. Ltd. stands at the forefront of educational and technological innovation, bridging the gap between learning and industry. We empower school students with hands-on exposure to emerging technologies such as Robotics & IOT, Drones & Aeromodelling, STEM, AR/VR and AI, nurturing curiosity and innovation from an early age.

For graduates and job seekers, we provide advanced skill training in Electric Vehicles, Battery Technology, PCB Designing & PCBA, Solar Energy Technology and Industrial Robotics – aligned with industry demands and employability standards.

Driven by a strong focus on Research & Development, Scirobnics is pioneering next-generation drone and mobility solutions that address real-world challenges in sustainability, safety, and smart cities.

Shaping the Innovators of Tomorrow

At Scirobnics, we are dedicated to empowering the next generation with hands-on experience in the technologies that are transforming the world. Our focus is on providing school students with practical learning in Robotics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and Drones, fostering creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.For graduates, we offer specialized, job-oriented training in Electric Vehicles, Drones, and Semiconductors, ensuring they are ready to excel in the fast-evolving tech industry.

With a strong commitment to research and development, we are pioneering the next wave of drone technology, addressing real-world challenges, and preparing India's workforce for a future driven by innovation.

To be a global leader in future technology education and innovation – empowering learners, institutions, and communities to shape a sustainable, tech-driven world.

MISSION

At Scirobnics, our mission is to inspire curiosity, foster innovation, and provide hands-on learning in emerging technologies such as Robotics, AI, Drones, EVs, Semiconductors, STEM, and AR/VR. We aim to bridge the gap between education and industry by:

  • Equipping students with future-ready skills from an early age.
  • Providing job-oriented training for graduates and professionals.
  • Collaborating with governments, corporates, and institutions to build Centers of Excellence and outreach programs.
  • Taking science and innovation to the last mile through our “Technology on Wheels” mobile laboratories, ensuring no learner is left behind.
  • Driving research and development to create real-world technology solutions for tomorrow’s challenges.
